Beach Mansion

Beach Mansion by the high-end property developer Emaar, is yet another beautiful waterfront addition to the highly anticipated area of the Emaar Beachfront. The new project will consist of 2 towers and provide a stunning view of the Dubai Marina, the Burj Al Arab, Ain Dubai and Palm Jumeirah. Meanwhile, tower 1 plans to have 37 levels (G+M+6P+ 37) and Tower 2 will house 20 floors (B+G+M+6P+20)

There is a wide range of properties of various sizes and layouts available for purchase, suitable to fit the taste and needs of any buyer. Thus, Beach Mansion is set to feature 3-4 bedroom townhouses, 1-4 bedroom apartments, 4-bedroom penthouses, and 4-bedroom penthouse duplexes. Meanwhile, the total area of residences ranges from 793 sq.ft. to 6,220 sq.ft. Interested buyers will be keen to learn that each property will boast a balcony of 99-1,820 sq.ft. that will make the most of the gorgeous surrounding views of the city and the sea.

As for key features of the development, townhouses will comprise an upper and a lower level with a maid’s room & bathroom, 3 balconies and a laundry room, alongside generous living and dining spaces. In fact, each residence in Beach Mansion, apart from 1 and 2-bedroom apartments, will feature a maid’s room with their own bathroom. At the same time, luxury penthouse duplexes will boast a family room and an office, which will not be available in other property types of the upcoming complex.
